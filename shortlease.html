PROPERTY DETAILS

We are proud to announce our new and promising project in the outskirts of the ever busy and hectic city of Nairobi. Our definition of modern living at fair price. The project will be located in Tinganga, Kiambu county. A 30 minutes drive from Nairobi CBD, 200m from the tarmac with public transport access, which mean easy commute to and from the city.
The area is near Sasini Coffee Plantation, Holy Rosary Catholic church, Tinganga Shopping Center and Bright star Junior Academy. Tinganga being an agricultural rich area, you are guaranteed to get farm-fresh produce at very cheap prices too.
Ground breaking done and the project currently underway, completion date will be in May 2022 and the project will house 12 one bedroom units and 4 two bedroom units.
